Why Update Your Magellan GPS?

  • Get access to the latest maps and roads
  • Enhanced performance and bug fixes
  • Updated Points of Interest (POI)
  • Improved navigation accuracy and safety

How to Update Your Device

  1. Click Update Now to go to the official update page.
  2. Download the Magellan Content Manager software.
  3. Connect your GPS to the computer via USB.
  4. Launch the software and follow the instructions.
  5. After installation, safely disconnect and restart your device.
